Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a folder?

Click the “Add Folder…” button in the menu bar menu and select your folder.

Why do I get a permission prompt?

Bar Pilot uses security-scoped bookmarks. Granting access once preserves permissions across relaunches.

How do I remove a folder?

Open the submenu for a folder in the menu bar and click “Remove”.
